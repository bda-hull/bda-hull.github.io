Short Bio

I am a PhD. Computer Science Student at the University of Hull, Yorkshire, UK. I did my Bachelor of Technology in Electronics and Communication Engineering from Amity University, Noida, India, where I was the University Topper and Gold Medalist. I have published various research papers in the area of Audio Signal Processing, Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning and also filed three patents. I have co-authored papers with reputed professors and academicians from across the globe, including Prof. (Dr.) Hui-Huang Hsu (Tamkang University, Taiwan), Prof. (Dr.) Sven-Hendrik Vo√ü (Beuth Hochschule fur Technik, Berlin, Germany) and Prof. (Dr.) Zhen Liu (Ningbo University, China) in the area of Signal Processing, Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence. I was also a Visiting Research Scholar at Tamkang University, Taiwan on a Research Project funded by the Ministry of Education, Taiwan. I have a sound understanding of the industry and have done various internships, including being a System Verilog HEP Trainee at Mentor Graphics, Noida, India (A Siemens Business). Writing is my passion, and I have authored a book "Thoughts of a Young Man", a collection of short poems, inspirational quotes and random thoughts, which was highly applauded by people, especially youngsters. I have received several honors and awards including, Gold Medal from Amity University, Noida, India, Shree Baljit Shastri Award for Best in Human and Traditional Values, India Book of Records Holder 2018, Merit Scholarship from Amity University, Noida, India, Letter of Appreciation from Ministry of HRD, Govt. of India, Amul Vidya Bhushan Award and the like.

Research Interests

I am presently researching in the area of Autonomous Prediction and Scheduling of O&M for Offshore Wind Farms through Machine Learning, Deep Learning and Artificial Intelligence. My research interests include, but are not limited to Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, Electronic Design & Automation and Signal Processing.
