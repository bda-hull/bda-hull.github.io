Our Research

Deep Learning

We apply deep learning in various research projects due to its suitability for feature learning and its ability to identify patterns in large and noisy datasets.

Read more

Evolutionary Algorithms

We investigate evolutionary algorithms that are inspired by biological models and allow for transparent execution and interpretable outputs.

Read more

Interactive Systems

Interactive systems can interact with people using text, speech and touch and can be applied in expert systems, chat-/robots or assistive technologies.

Read more

Natural Language Processing

Our strengths in NLP lie in natural language generation, (spoken) dialogue systems, sentiment analysis and computational linguistics.

Read more

Biology and Health

We have applied evolutionary algorithms in the medical domain, e.g. for gait control and kinematics, Parkinson's disease and we research interactive systems for dementia.

Read more

Data and the Natural World

We apply deep learning and evolutionary algorithms in projects with natural sciences including biology and chemistry.

Read more
