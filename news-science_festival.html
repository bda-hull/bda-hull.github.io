British Science Festival

David Benoit, Senior Lecturer in Molecular Physics, and Nina Dethlefs gave a talk at the British Science Festival -- Let the right AI in.

We discussed the techniques and methods behind modern AI systems, neural networks / deep learning, some of the most popular, well known and successful applications and also some of the limitations that current systems face, including their limited semantic understanding of the world and possible ethical implications.
